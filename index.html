About VITS, Satna

The Most Trusted Group of Vindhya Region

More than 23 Years of Academic Experience That You Can Trust

With the aim to introduce and promote higher education in Vindhya region of Madhya Pradesh, DPR Charitable Trust came into existence in 2001. In 2002, it established the first private engineering institute of the region in Satna. Over the years, various new courses were added to meet the rising aspirations of the youth. Presently, 6 institutions of higher education and a CBSE-affiliated school are successfully serving society under the umbrella of DPR Charitable Trust.

The lush green campus is spread over 25 acres with 5 Lac Sq. Ft. of built-up area. It features a Student Activity Centre with indoor sports facilities, a stadium for outdoor sports, an air-conditioned auditorium seating 250 students, an auditorium with 800-seat capacity, and a boys' hostel accommodating 200 students. All institutes have modern state-of-the-art infrastructure and well-equipped laboratories.
